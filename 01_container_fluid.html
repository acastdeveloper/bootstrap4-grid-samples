Notes:

The classes container and container-fluid are required. Both are containers of rows and they delimite maximum width of contents, being container-fluid as wider as the browser's window is. Meanwhile class container is as wider as the max-width defined even is less than the browser's window.

The class row is the father element of the columns' classes.

The class col is the more elemental defined class for columns. It's used for defining width of columns in extra small devices(less than 576px).
